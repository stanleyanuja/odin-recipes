Cauliflower Pickle

A healthy delicious South Indian pickle

Are you a working professional looking to pickle and store delicious food to save those extra minutes on a busy workday morning?

This South Indian pickle is right for you. Once made, this can be stored up to one month without compromising on the taste. Take a deep dive into making the tasty Cauliflower pickle

  • Ingredients

    • Cauliflower Florets - 2 cups
    • Mustard powder - 1 cup
    • Red chili powder - 1/2 cup
    • Salt - 1/4 cup
    • Oil - 1/2 cup
    • Lemon juice - 1

  • Steps

    1. Clean the cauliflower and cut the florets to the desired size
    2. Soak in salted luke warm water for 10 mins
    3. Wash them under fresh running water, drain and set aside. If you chance to sun dry do it for 2 â€“ 3 hrs. If you cannot sun dry, then dry them on cotton cloth and fan dry or leave it for over night
    4. Make sure there is no water in the florets
    5. Powder the mustard seeds and keep them aside.(or use store bought mustard powder)
    6. In a mixing bowl, add dried cauliflower florets
    7. Add mustard powder, red chili powder, salt, oil and pour lemon juice
    8. Mix everything well, leave it for 1 day
    9. On next day oil should be floating on top. If not then add more oil as needed
    10. Taste the pickle and add little salt if needed
    11. Stays good for about a month in refrigerator
    12. Serve cauliflower pickle with rice and ghee
